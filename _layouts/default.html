Make it your own.

Host your own instance of 2cloud. Own your data.

We open source all of the software running on 2cloud.org under the the MIT license, and we strive to make it simple to administer your own data.

We think it's important that you have the option to own your data completely. We offer a hosted instance for the users that don't have the technical skill or desire to run their own instance, but we absolutely refuse to lock people into using it.
